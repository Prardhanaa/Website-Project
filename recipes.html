Butter Chicken

Prep: 20m • Cook: 40m • Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp chili powder, salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp yogurt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes (optional).
  2. Pan-sear chicken until lightly browned and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter, add tomato puree and spices; cook 8–10 minutes.
  4. Add cream, return chicken to the sauce, simmer 10–12 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Finish with garam masala and a knob of butter. Serve with naan or rice.

Palak Paneer

Prep: 15m • Cook: 25m • Serves 3-4

Ingredients

  • 300g spinach (palak), cleaned
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato, ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp garam masala

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach, blend to a smooth puree.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices; add spinach puree and simmer.
  3. Add paneer cubes and cook 4–5 minutes. Finish with a drizzle of cream (optional).

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Prep: 10m • Cook: 30-40m • Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas (or canned)
  • 1 large onion, 2 tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chole masala (or garam masala + ground coriander)
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder) or a squeeze of lemon

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, add ginger-garlic and tomatoes until soft.
  2. Add spices and chickpeas with a splash of water; simmer 15–20 minutes.
  3. Adjust tanginess with amchur or lemon. Garnish with coriander.

Tags: Main Course

Hyderabadi Biryani

Prep: 25m • Cook: 1h • Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice, 500g chicken (or vegetables)
  • yogurt, fried onions, biryani masala, mint, coriander
  • 1 tsp saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Par-cook rice; marinate chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Layer rice and chicken in a heavy pot, add fried onions, saffron milk, and seal lid.
  3. Cook on low heat (dum) for 25–35 minutes until aromatic and cooked.

Masala Dosa

Prep: 20m (batter longer) • Cook: 20m • Serves 3-4

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter (rice + urad dal, fermented) or store-bought
  • 3 medium potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies

Instructions

  1. Prepare potato masala: sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions and add boiled, mashed potatoes and spices.
  2. Spread dosa batter on hot skillet, place filling inside, fold and serve with chutney and sambar.

Samosa

Prep: 30m • Cook: 20m • Makes 8–10

Ingredients

  • For dough: 2 cups all-purpose flour, oil, salt
  • Filling: 3 potatoes, peas, cumin, garam masala

Instructions

  1. Make firm dough and rest. Prepare spiced potato filling.
  2. Roll dough into small circles, fill, seal and deep-fry until golden.

Tags: Snacks

Desserts

Gulab Jamun

Prep: 20m • Cook: 20m • Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 250g milk powder or khoya, 1 cup flour, sugar syrup flavored with cardamom

Instructions

  1. Make soft dough from milk solids/flour, shape into small balls. Fry on low heat until dark golden.
  2. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
