Packing Light: My Capsule List

March 18, 2025 · 5 min read

Traveling light is a game-changer. Here’s my capsule wardrobe list for two weeks, all fitting into a carry-on bag.

Clothing

  • 3 T-shirts
  • 2 Long-sleeve shirts
  • 2 Pairs of pants
  • 1 Lightweight jacket
  • 5 Pairs of underwear

Essentials

  • Toiletries kit
  • Phone charger and adapters
  • Travel documents

Keep it simple, stay comfortable, and enjoy the journey!
